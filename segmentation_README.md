## 🧠 Business Concepts

### K-Means Clustering (Simulated)
Groups 500 customers into 5 clusters based on:
- **Monthly Spend** (Monetary)
- **Orders per Month** (Frequency)
- **Days Since Last Order** (Recency)

### RFM Analysis
Each segment scored on:
- **R** — How recently did they buy?
- **F** — How often do they buy?
- **M** — How much do they spend?

### Segments Defined

| Segment | Strategy |
|---|---|
| 👑 Champions | VIP rewards + upsell |
| 💎 Loyalists | Subscription plans + bundles |
| 🌱 Potentials | Onboarding + flash sales |
| ⚠️ At Risk | Win-back campaign |
| 😴 Hibernating | Re-engagement push |
